## Mental Health Analysis Project

## 📌 Objective

Analyze factors affecting mental health in the workplace and understand patterns behind treatment-seeking behavior.

---

## 📊 Key Insights

### 🔹 Do Benefits Increase Help-Seeking?

Employees with access to mental health benefits are significantly more likely to seek treatment (~65%) compared to those without benefits (~48%) or unaware (~37%).
This highlights the importance of both **availability and awareness of benefits**.

---

### 🔹 Are Employees Afraid to Talk?

A large proportion of employees are hesitant or unsure about seeking help.
While some feel comfortable discussing mental health with coworkers, fewer feel the same with supervisors, indicating **workplace stigma and hierarchical barriers**.

---

### 🔹 Does Mental Health Affect Work?

Mental health significantly impacts productivity, with many employees reporting interference “sometimes” or “often.”
Treatment-seeking increases sharply when work is affected, showing **reactive rather than proactive behavior**.
